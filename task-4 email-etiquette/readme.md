## 🌐 Part 2: Social Media Awareness

This section provides guidelines for using social media responsibly.  
📌 Based on the checklist document: :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}  

---

### ✅ Best Practices (Do’s)

- Maintain a professional profile  
- Share achievements and skills  
- Respect others’ privacy  
- Log out from shared devices  
- Regularly check privacy settings  

---

### ❌ Things to Avoid (Don’ts)

- Posting inappropriate or risky content  
- Oversharing personal problems online  
- Accepting unknown friend requests  
- Leaving accounts open on public devices  
- Giving unnecessary app permissions
