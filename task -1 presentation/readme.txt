# 🌐 Youthful Digital Literacy

A simple and beginner-friendly project to spread awareness about **digital literacy** among students.

## 📖 What is Digital Literacy?

Digital literacy means having the ability to:
- Find information online 🔍  
- Evaluate if the information is trustworthy ✅  
- Communicate effectively using digital platforms 💬  

In today’s world, it’s an essential skill for students and professionals alike.

## 🛠️ Useful Digital Tools for Students

Here are some helpful tools that can improve productivity:

- **Notion** – Organize notes, tasks, and projects  
- **Google Docs** – Create and collaborate on documents  
- **Trello** – Manage tasks and track progress visually  

## 🔐 Safe Internet Practices

Staying safe online is just as important as being productive:

- Use strong and unique passwords 🔑  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links ⚠️  
- Protect your personal information 🛡️

This infographic aims to:
